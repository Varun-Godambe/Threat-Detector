About LogSynth

LogSynth is an advanced analysis tool designed to help security professionals and developers identify potential threats within various digital assets. By leveraging simulated AI agents, it provides quick and insightful analysis directly in your browser.

Core Features

  • File Analysis: Upload scripts (PowerShell, Python), configuration files (JSON), or plain text files. The Static Analysis Agent will scan for vulnerabilities, dangerous commands, and misconfigurations.
  • Log Analysis: Drop log files to detect anomalies. Agents will look for patterns like automated behavior, suspicious simultaneous logins, and unusual endpoint usage to construct a potential attack chain.
  • URL Scanning: Enter a URL to check it against a database of malicious domains and analyze its structure for signs of phishing or other web-based threats.

Privacy First

All analysis is performed entirely on your computer (client-side). Your files and data are never uploaded to any server, ensuring your information remains private and secure.
